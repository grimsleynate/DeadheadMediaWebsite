About

The story of us.

  • 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Founded in 2017, we are a small web design agency based in Ogemaw County, Michigan. Over the last couple of years, we have developed a reputation for building websites that look amazing and are easy-to-use.

  • September 2017

    A Trip to University

    Shortly after starting the agency, our lead developer decided to formalize his skills by persuing a degree. He is currently attending the University of Advancing Technology to obtain a Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science, with an expected graduation date of 2019.

  • March 2018

    Transition to Full Service

    After testing the waters, we finally decided to become a full service web agency. In March of 2018, we transitioned to a full-service agency capable of prototyping, creating, and deploying web pages. We also began offering full CMS services, eCommerce plug-ins, SEO support, and fully responsive designs. If you can dream it, we can build it!
